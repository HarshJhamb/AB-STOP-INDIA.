Who We Are

The STOP India Legacy

STOP India has been aiming high since its founding and today stands as one of India's leading exhibition logistics companies with a truly worldwide reach. The idea of S.T.O.P. — Sustainable Transport Organization of Professionals — originated in 2001, beginning as an alternative tourism service.

The journey diversified in 2012 to seamlessly move both people and cargo across borders. STOP India is now recognised as one of the few Indian forwarders specialising in true customer-first service — handling time-critical cargo for global trade shows with zero delays and complete reliability.

With over 25 years of industry expertise, every client is at the heart of everything we do. Our customers don't just hire a logistics company — they gain a trusted partner invested in their success on the world stage.

Our Journey

Milestones That
Defined Us

A timeline of pivotal moments that shaped STOP India into a global industry leader.

1998
The founding vision takes shape — a belief that Indian businesses deserve world-class logistics support for international exhibitions.
2001
S.T.O.P. — Sustainable Transport Organization of Professionals — is formally conceptualized, beginning with alternative tourism services.
2012
STOP India officially registers as a dedicated exhibition logistics provider, expanding operations to cover cargo movement across major Indian cities.
2015
International expansion accelerates with partnerships established in 30+ countries, cementing our footprint as a genuinely global player.
2018
A landmark year: 500+ exhibitions handled annually with a zero-delay record. Industry recognition and awards begin to follow our growing reputation.
2021
Advanced real-time tracking systems and 24/7 support operations are fully launched, transforming the client experience across the globe.
2023
STOP India reaches 50+ countries with dedicated on-site handling partners. Recognised as India's preferred exhibition logistics leader by the industry.
