Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything sports exhibitors need to know about moving gear to events worldwide.

How quickly can you transport sports equipment to urgent events?

We specialize in rapid transport. For urgent shipments, we offer express air freight with same-day departure from major Indian cities. We coordinate with airline cargo partners for fastest routing and can arrange express customs clearance at destination airports. For time-critical sports events, we've delivered equipment within 48-72 hours globally, including customs clearance.

Can you handle heavy fitness equipment and large machinery?

Yes, absolutely. We have extensive experience with heavy fitness equipment—treadmills, weight machines, cardio equipment, and specialized sports machinery. We use reinforced crating, heavy-duty pallets, and equipment-specific packaging. For items weighing several tonnes, we arrange flatbed transport with load-restraint certification. On-site installation crews handle unloading, assembly, and technical setup with proper safety protocols.

Do you provide assembly and installation support at venues?

Yes. We provide full assembly and installation support at your venue. Our trained crew handles unloading, unpacking, assembly of display structures, and technical setup of equipment. For complex installations or large displays, we coordinate with venue technical teams and can provide 24/7 support throughout event setup. This is especially valuable for time-critical sports events where rapid setup is essential.

How do you protect premium sports brands and expensive equipment during transport?

We use sport-grade protective packaging: reinforced boxes, shock-absorbing foam, moisture barriers, and secure fastening. For high-value items, we offer temperature and humidity monitoring. All shipments are insured, and we provide damage documentation and photo records. For premium brand merchandise, we ensure display-ready delivery maintaining retail presentation standards throughout transport and handling.

Can you handle multi-country sports logistics for international events?

Yes. We coordinate complex multi-country logistics for international sports events. We manage export documentation, cross-border customs clearance through multiple countries, and coordinate with local partners at each destination. Our global network in 50+ countries ensures seamless hand-offs and unified tracking throughout the entire journey, from India to your final venue.

How far in advance should we book for major sports events?

For major international sports events, we recommend booking 10–14 weeks in advance to secure capacity, arrange optimal shipping, and complete all customs documentation. For urgent events or rush shipments, we can accommodate at short notice with express rates. Our real-time tracking system allows live visibility of your shipment status from dispatch to venue arrival.
