From Warehouse to Exhibition Stand

A single-point exhibition logistics partner managing every step between your warehouse and your exhibition stand.

Pre-Show Planning & Consultation

We start with timelines, venue regulations, and shipment strategy. Our team coordinates with organizers and stand builders.

Pickup from Your Warehouse

Our team collects material from your factory or warehouse with professional packing support if required.

Documentation & Compliance

We prepare all documentation, ATA Carnet or temporary import papers ensuring full compliance with regulations.

International Freight Movement

Your shipment moves by air, sea or road through our global partner network with real-time tracking.

On-Site Delivery to Stand

At destination, our on-site team clears cargo, delivers to your stand, and manages empty case storage during the show.

Return & Post-Show Handling

After the show, we repack, re-label and manage return shipments or onward movement to the next exhibition.
